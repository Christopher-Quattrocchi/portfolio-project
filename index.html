This is me!

I am an aspiring developer based in Portland, OR. I hope to graduate from Epicodus in March of 2024, and to begin work shortly after. I have an AS degree from Portland State University. I don't currently have professional experience in tech, but I hope to change that very soon! I've had an interest in tech for a while, and I enjoy building things. I see Epicodus as a path to fulfilling my dreams. I love the outdoors, and enjoy spending time hiking and running in nature. I've worked as a personal trainer, a cook, a real estate assistant, in retail, as a doordash driver, and as an educational assistent for students with special needs. I'm looking to start a fulfilling, dynamic career and believe that tech is my calling.
